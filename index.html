About me

Anner Turner is the poster child for professional support and executive administrative finesse. Born and raised in the big city of Decatur, Alabama, Anner moved to Huntsville, Alabama to pursue her professional endeavors. She has over ten years of knowledge, wisdom and experience gained when it comes to supporting high-profiled business executives and public servants in making a difference. Her passion for helping, supporting and serving others lead her to join numerous professional organizations including the National Association of Professional Women, the International Association of Professional Women, the Society of Human Resource, the National Contract Management Association, the American Society of Administrative Professional and the Order of the Eastern Star. Anner has extended her professional arsenal to include entrepreneurship as she launched her virtual assisting agency in early 2020. Her focus is supporting busy professionals with day to day task fulfillment so that they can focus on more important business functions.
